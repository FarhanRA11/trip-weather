Notes

  • It will get more accurate result if trip is >1hour or >30km
  • Maximum trip is 3days or 2160km, otherwise the result may not be found
  • The more famous a place is, the more accurate the results may be
  • The further the distance, the less accurate the weather results will be
  • Using the shortest (not the fastest) route
  • Not 100% accurate, the realworld time and weather may be the opposite from the result
  • Some districts/cities/provinces/countries name may misspelled or maybe in wrong order
